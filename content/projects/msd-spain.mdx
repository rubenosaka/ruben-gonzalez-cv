# MSD España - Corporate Website

Corporate website for MSD España, a leading pharmaceutical company. The project involved content management and migration to WordPress, ensuring a modern and accessible web presence for healthcare professionals and patients.

## Project Overview

MSD España's corporate website serves as the primary digital touchpoint for healthcare professionals, patients, and stakeholders in Spain. The project focused on content management, migration, and maintaining a professional web presence that aligns with MSD's global standards.

## Key Features

### Content Management

- **WordPress Platform**: Robust content management system
- **Multi-language Support**: Spanish and regional language variations
- **Content Migration**: Seamless transition from legacy systems
- **Editorial Workflow**: Streamlined content publishing process

### User Experience

- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for all devices and screen sizes
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 compliance for inclusive design
- **Performance**: Fast loading times and optimized user experience
- **SEO Optimization**: Search engine friendly structure

- **Patient Information**: Educational content and health resources
